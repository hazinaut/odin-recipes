Ful

Growing up we would make ful every Sunday. This family time meal is a favorite of our and very traditional to our culture.

Ingredients

  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/2 Lime
  • 1 Can of Ful
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Put can of ful in pot and place on medium heat stove.
  2. Once boiliing, take off heat and put beans in bow.l
  3. Mix water from pot into bowl until desired consistency.
  4. Enjoy with fresh veggies or hard boiled eggs
